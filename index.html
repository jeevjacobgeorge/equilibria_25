About Equilibria

Equilibria is a national-level symposium, hosted annually by the Department of Biotechnology and Biochemical Engineering (BT & BCE) at Sree chitra thirunal college of engineering, Trivandrum under the banner of EVOGEN, which encompasses both the staff and student bodies of Biotechnology department. The symposium features paper and poster presentations, talk sessions and workshops. Equilibria facilitates the exchange of ideas and expertise among intellectuals, researchers, and students in the fields of biotechnology and biochemical engineering.

Our Goals

The primary goal of this conference is to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange among professionals working in various domains of biotechnology. Through keynote speeches, panel discussions, and interactive sessions, attendees will gain insights into the latest research trends and technological innovations in cross-disciplinary fields of biotechnology .

Event FAQs

Who can participate in the conference ?

The symposium is open to undergraduate and postgraduate students, researchers, academicians, and professionals related to the field of biotechnology and allied engineering branches.

Will there be any workshops or competitions during the symposium?

Yes, we will be organizing workshops, competitions, and other interactive sessions. Details about these events will be updated on our website soon.

How do i get to the conference venue?

Directions and transportation options are available in the "Venue Information" section of the website. We also recommend using [Local Transport Service] for convenient travel.

Can I register onsite?

Yes, on-site registration is available for participation only. However, please note that on-site registration does not include the option to present. If you wish to attend as a participant, you can register upon arrival, but all presentation slots must be secured in advance.

What are the accomodation options available near the conference venue?

The accommodation for the delegates can be arranged in hotels and hostels subject to the availability of the same on request and on payment basis Please contact the organizing committee for more details and rates.
